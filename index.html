Testament

I, John of IBC, leave my entire inheritance to my bastard daughter, whose name is censored, to whom I owe thousands of apologies. As a way of repairing my mistakes, everything I built during my life will be passed on to her. I really wish her can forgive me, but I understand if her don't. About him

Born and raised in Caps/MG, son of coffee farming parents, John became involved in the family business from an early age, bringing new ideas, ways of working and high power in negotiating grains. He made a fortune and created enemies in the region. With high political influence, he presided over the Brazilian Coffee Institute for 15 years. A powerful and honorable man, he was a bohemian and had four daughters with several women, but he never got along so well with family matters. This dramatically earned him a lonely death.
